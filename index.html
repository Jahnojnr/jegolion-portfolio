Who Is He

The Man Behind the Laugh

Born Nwankwo Uchechukwu and known everywhere as Jegolion, he is one of Nigeria's most naturally gifted comedic talents — a man whose deadpan expressions crack audiences without a single forced moment. But behind the laughter is a sharp, purpose-driven executive.

A key figure within the Pamtech Group ecosystem, Jegolion has made a seamless transition from content creator to corporate representative — negotiating multimillion-naira deals, leading automotive operations, and carrying the group's "Billionaire Mechanic" brand across Nigeria and beyond.

His story is Pamtech's proof of concept: build the person first, and the vision builds itself.
